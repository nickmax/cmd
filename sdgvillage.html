SDG VILLAGE

What is the SDG Village?

The SDG Village is a dedicated space at PDF for exploring the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) through dynamic conversations and interactive sessions. It brings together diverse participants to discuss local implementation strategies, share experiences, and assess progress towards global sustainability commitments. Conversations in the SDG Village cover a wide range of topics, including youth engagement, climate action, value-based education, crime prevention, and fashion's role in advancing sustainability. It's a collaborative space for driving innovative solutions that contribute to a more sustainable and equitable future for Kenya and beyond.
