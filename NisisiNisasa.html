Ni Sisi Ni Sasa Village

What is the Ni Sisi Ni Sasa Village?

The Ni Sisi Ni Sasa Village at the People’s Dialogue Festival is a dynamic space designed to empower high school students by immersing them in critical conversations about governance, democracy, and their role in shaping the future. Rooted in the belief that youth are not just leaders of tomorrow but active participants today, this village serves as a platform for meaningful dialogue, experience-sharing, and informed policy discussions. Through interactive and engaging sessions, students will explore contemporary global and national issues, critically analyze governance structures, and develop the skills necessary to contribute effectively to democratic processes.

At the heart of the Ni Sisi Ni Sasa Village is the principle "Nihil de Nobis, sine Nobis" (Nothing About Us Without Us)—a commitment to ensuring that young voices are not only heard but actively influence decisions that shape their lives. The village features a variety of interactive formats, including debates, expert-led masterclasses, fireside chats, democratic games, and thought-provoking panel discussions. By bridging the gap between theory and practice, the village fosters civic responsibility, political awareness, and leadership among students, preparing them to be informed citizens who drive meaningful change in their communities and beyond.
