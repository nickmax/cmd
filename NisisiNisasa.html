Ni Sisi Ni Sasa Village

What is the Ni Sisi Ni Sasa Village?

The Ni Sisi Ni Sasa Village is a vibrant space for participants under 18 to engage in age-appropriate, thought-provoking discussions on key issues shaping their future. It provides young people with an opportunity to explore topics like youth empowerment in national policy, the digital divide, gender equality, mental health, civic engagement, and more. With sessions led by experts, the village encourages youth to become active participants in shaping Kenya's future, promoting leadership, legal awareness, and community action.
