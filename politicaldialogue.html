POLITICAL DIALOGUE VILLAGE

What is the Political Dialogue Village?

The Political Dialogue Village provides a platform for political actors, including elected and nominated leaders, parliamentary caucuses, government agencies, political parties, and citizens, to engage in constructive, issue-based discussions. Focused on inter-party dialogue, it fosters debates on governance, inclusivity, and reforms needed to strengthen Kenya's democratic processes. This village serves as a space for diverse political voices to collaborate and tackle the country's most pressing political challenges.
