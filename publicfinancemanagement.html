PUBLIC FINANCE MANAGEMENT VILLAGE

What is the PFM Village?

The Public Finance Management (PFM) Village, hosted by Budget Talk Global (BTG), is a dedicated dialogue space within the People’s Dialogue Festival (PDF) aimed at fostering discussions on public finance and governance. Since 2021, BTG has engaged in public finance conversations within the SDGs Village at PDF, but recognizing the need for a more focused platform, it established the PFM Village in 2025. The village provides a space for in-depth conversations on issues such as budget financing, taxation, service delivery, devolution, and social accountability, bringing together national and county government actors, civil society organizations, media, private sector stakeholders, and citizens. The inaugural PFM Village was themed “From Public Budgets to Public Benefits,” emphasizing the role of public finance in sustainable development and policy decision-making.

The PFM Village aims to institutionalize public finance discussions within the PDF, strengthen collaborations among stakeholders, and raise awareness about how public finance shapes governance and society. Through various activities such as expert panel discussions, open mic sessions, debates, masterclasses, and digital advocacy, the village educates citizens on budget processes and their influence on policy decisions. It also serves as a networking hub for organizations, policymakers, and the public to engage in meaningful discourse and forge partnerships. Ultimately, the PFM Village empowers citizens to participate in budget-making processes and advocates for inclusive and transparent public finance management in Kenya and beyond.
