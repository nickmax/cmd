PUBLIC FINANCE MANAGEMENT VILLAGE

What is the PFM Village?

The PFM (Public Finance Management) Village at PDF is a platform focused on discussions around the effective and transparent management of public resources. It provides a space for citizens, government officials, and experts to explore strategies for fiscal accountability, budgetary reforms, and citizen engagement in financial governance. Topics in the PFM Village include sustainable economic development, improving public sector accountability, and ensuring transparency in the allocation and use of public funds for national development.
