YOUTH VILLAGE

What is the Youth Village?

The Youth Village, at the People Dialogue Festival, is a key segment of the Democracy Festival that is designed to provide a platform for young adults (18-35) to engage in discussions, share experiences and actively take part in conversations centred on issues that affect them. Through the Youth Village, young people engage in thought-provoking discussions and activities centred on key issues affecting their generation, with topics ranging from enhancing youth participation in governance, socio-economic empowerment, and inclusive leadership. The village provides room for young people, drawn from all sectors and regions of the world to dialogue on critical societal issues, and to meaningfully register their demand for inclusive, accountable and value- driven governance, all in the promotion of collaborative efforts for Global sustainable development. Through a collaborative approach, the platform enables both youth-serving, youth-led, grassroots organizations and stakeholders all across, to co-develop themes and topics that align with young people’s needs and aspirations, through panel discussions, keynote addresses, fireside chats, break out – mentorship sessions, debates, artistic and creative performances like spoken words, poetry, live murals, intergenerational dialogue and gamification of civic education, the village ensures that dialogue among young people remains relevant and impactful, addressing priority issues in governance, social equity, and sustainable development.
